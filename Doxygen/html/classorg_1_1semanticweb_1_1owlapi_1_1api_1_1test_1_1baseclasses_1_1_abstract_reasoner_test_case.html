Descripción detallada

Author: Matthew Horridge
The University of Manchester
Bio-Health Informatics Group
Date: 08-Jul-2010

This test case creates a small ontology and tests the getters in the reasoner interface. The test ontology isn't designed to test the correctness of reasoning results, rather it is designed to test the reasoner returns the results in the form required by the OWL API reasoner interface.
