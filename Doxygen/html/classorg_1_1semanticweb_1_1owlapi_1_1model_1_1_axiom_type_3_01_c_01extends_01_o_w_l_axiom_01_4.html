Descripción detallada

Author: Matthew Horridge
The University Of Manchester
Bio-Health Informatics Group
Date: 27-Jul-2007

Represents the type of axioms which can belong to ontologies. Axioms can be retrieved from ontologies by their AxiomType. For example, see org.semanticweb.owlapi.model.OWLOntology#getAxioms(AxiomType) and org.semanticweb.owlapi.model.OWLOntology#getAxiomCount(AxiomType, boolean).

Devuelve
